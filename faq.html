Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Semper Deep's veteran investment platform and services.

Semper Deep is an investment platform specifically designed for veterans and military families. We provide access to exclusive investment opportunities in defense technology, real estate, and venture capital. Our platform combines military-grade security with investment expertise to help veterans build wealth and secure their financial future.

We specialize in three main areas:

  • Defense Technology: Investments in cutting-edge defense contractors and military tech startups
  • Real Estate: Vetted residential and commercial properties with strong growth potential
  • Venture Capital: Early-stage investments in veteran-founded and military-related businesses

All opportunities are carefully vetted by our team of veteran investment specialists.

Investment minimums vary by opportunity type:

  • Defense Technology Portfolio: $10,000 minimum
  • Real Estate Opportunities: $25,000 minimum
  • Venture Capital Fund: $50,000 minimum
  • Diversified Veteran Portfolio: $5,000 minimum

We also offer educational resources and planning tools for investors at any level.

We use secure verification through military records and DD-214 documentation. The process is completely confidential and typically takes 24-48 hours. Family members of veterans may also qualify for certain investment opportunities with appropriate documentation.

Our fee structure is transparent and competitive:

  • Advisory Fee: 1% annually on managed assets
  • Performance Fee: 20% of profits above 8% annual returns
  • Transaction Fees: Vary by investment type, typically 0.5-2%

Veterans receive a 25% discount on all advisory fees for the first year.

  • Real-time portfolio values and performance
  • Risk analysis and diversification metrics
  • Monthly detailed reports
  • Mobile app for on-the-go monitoring
  • Direct communication with your advisor
